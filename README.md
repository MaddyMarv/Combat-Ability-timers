### [Ability Timer](https://www.nexusmods.com/warhammer40kdarktide/mods/673)
**The short version:** Adds a visual timer to your combat ability so you know exactly when it ends.

**The details:** Standalone mod that adds a clean timer to your HUD that counts down the exact duration of your active ability. Sometimes it's hard to tell when your ability is going to run out in the middle of a horde. It works for all classes and takes the guesswork out of timing your pushes and dodges.
